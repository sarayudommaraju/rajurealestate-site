Who we are

Raju Real Estate is a boutique advisory built on a simple promise: every property we put in front of you is verified, and every step from shortlist to registration has one accountable point of contact.

We operate on the ground in four South Indian markets. For a buyer sitting in Dubai, New Jersey or Singapore, that local presence is everything: verified site visits on video, sharper pricing, and deals that close without last-minute surprises at the sub-registrar office.
