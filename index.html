How we protect you from title fraud

The number one risk in Indian property is a bad title. Before any listing reaches you, it clears a documented due-diligence check. You get the paperwork, not just our word.

01

Layout & approvals

HMDA, DTCP, BMRDA or RERA approval numbers checked against the sanctioned plan.

02

Clean title chain

Ownership traced back through link documents, with no unexplained gaps.

03

Encumbrance certificate

A current EC covering 13 to 15 years, so no hidden mortgage or lien survives.

04

Legal opinion

A lawyer's title opinion and boundary survey, available to you on request.

Questions buyers ask us

Can I buy from abroad without visiting?

Yes. We run live video site visits, handle the paperwork, and arrange power of attorney so you can complete the purchase and registration without flying down.

How do you make sure a property has a clean title?

Every listing clears a documented check: layout and RERA/HMDA/DTCP approvals, the title chain, a 13 to 15 year encumbrance certificate, and a lawyer's opinion. You get the paperwork, not just our word.

Do you help first-time and local buyers too?

Absolutely. We work with families buying their first home, people looking for apartments or rentals, plot buyers and investors alike, across all four cities.

Do you help with home loans and registration?

Yes. We coordinate home-loan options with lenders and manage registration and handover end to end, so it's one point of contact for the whole deal.
